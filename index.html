Frequently Asked Questions

What services does BizMovePro offer?

We offer comprehensive office relocation services including packing, transportation, setup assistance, and real-time tracking. Our services are designed to minimize downtime and ensure a smooth transition for your business.

How can I track my move?

You can track your move in real-time via our customer dashboard which provides live updates on the location of your items. We also send SMS and email notifications at key milestones during the moving process.

Do you offer weekend moving services?

Yes, we specialize in weekend and overnight moving services to minimize disruption to your business operations. Our team is available 24/7 to accommodate your schedule.

How far in advance should I book my move?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for optimal scheduling. However, we do accommodate last-minute moves when possible, especially for our premium clients.

What safety measures do you follow during the move?

We ensure the safety of your items by using high-quality packing materials, trained professionals, and secure transportation. Additionally, we offer insurance options for added peace of mind.
